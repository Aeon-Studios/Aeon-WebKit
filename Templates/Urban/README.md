# Urban Street Photography Template

## Edgy, Bold Modern Photography Portfolio

Contemporary urban template with bold graphics and strong grid layouts. Perfect for street photographers, urban explorers, and edgy creative brands.

### 🎨 Design Features
- **Edgy Bold Design**: Strong visual impact
- **Dark Modern Visuals**: Dark sophisticated theme
- **Strong Grid Layouts**: Structured composition
- **High Contrast**: Bold type & imagery
- **Urban Energy**: Contemporary feel
- **Fully Responsive**: Mobile optimized

### 📋 Pages Included
- `index.html` - Bold hero with featured work
- `gallery.html` - Grid portfolio showcase
- `contact.html` - Contact form
- `assets/` - CSS, JavaScript, image placeholders

### 🎯 Customization Guide

#### Photographer/Brand Name
In `index.html`:
```html
<!-- CUSTOMIZE: Your name -->
<h1 class="photographer-name">Urban Photographer</h1>
```

#### Logo Upload
```html
<!-- CUSTOMIZE: Your logo -->
<img src="assets/images/logo.png" class="logo">
```

#### Portfolio Images
In `gallery.html`:
```html
<!-- CUSTOMIZE: Urban street photos -->
<img src="assets/images/urban-1.jpg" class="urban-photo">
```

#### Color Scheme
In `assets/css/style.css`:
```css
:root {
  --primary: #1a1a1a;
  --accent: #FF00FF;
  --highlight: #00FFFF;
  --bg: #0a0a0a;
}
```

#### Bio Section
In `index.html`:
```html
<!-- CUSTOMIZE: Your story -->
<p class="bio">Capturing urban life...</p>
```

#### Themes/Specialties
In `index.html`:
```html
<!-- CUSTOMIZE: Your photography themes -->
<tag>Street</tag>
<tag>Urban</tag>
<tag>Architecture</tag>
```

#### Contact Info
In `contact.html`:
```html
<!-- CUSTOMIZE: Your email -->
<form action="mailto:urban@example.com">
```
