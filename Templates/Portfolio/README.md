# Personal Portfolio Template

## Solo Creator Photography Portfolio

Simple, focused template for individual photographers and solo creators. Emphasis on personal branding, bio, and gallery.

### 🎨 Design Features
- **Simple Solo Focus**: Clean, uncluttered design
- **Bio + Gallery**: Emphasis on story and work
- **Personal Branding**: Professional appearance
- **Easy Navigation**: Minimal menu
- **Image Focused**: Gallery takes center stage
- **Mobile Optimized**: Fully responsive

### 📋 Pages Included
- `index.html` - Personal landing page
- `gallery.html` - Photo gallery
- `contact.html` - Simple contact form
- `assets/` - CSS, JavaScript, image placeholders

### 🎯 Customization Guide

#### Your Name
In `index.html`:
```html
<!-- CUSTOMIZE: Your name -->
<h1 class="name">Your Name</h1>
```

#### Profile Photo
```html
<!-- CUSTOMIZE: Your profile photo -->
<img src="assets/images/profile.jpg" class="profile-photo">
```

#### Bio
In `index.html`:
```html
<!-- CUSTOMIZE: Your bio -->
<p class="bio">
  I am a photographer specializing in...
</p>
```

#### Gallery Images
In `gallery.html`:
```html
<!-- CUSTOMIZE: Your photos -->
<img src="assets/images/photo-1.jpg" alt="Portfolio">
```

#### Colors
In `assets/css/style.css`:
```css
:root {
  --primary: #333;
  --accent: #666;
  --bg: #fff;
}
```

#### Skills/Specialties
In `index.html`:
```html
<!-- CUSTOMIZE: Your specialties -->
<ul class="skills">
  <li>Landscape</li>
  <li>Portrait</li>
</ul>
```

#### Social Links
```html
<!-- CUSTOMIZE: Your profiles -->
<a href="https://instagram.com/yourname">Instagram</a>
```

#### Contact Email
In `contact.html`:
```html
<!-- CUSTOMIZE: Your email -->
<form action="mailto:hello@example.com">
```
